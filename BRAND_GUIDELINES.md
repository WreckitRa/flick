## Component Guidelines

### Buttons
- **Minimum Height**: 48px (comfortable touch)
- **Padding**: 16px horizontal, 12px vertical
- **Border**: 3px solid (darker shade)
- **Border Bottom**: 4-5px (3D effect)
- **Press State**: Scale to 96% with haptic
- **Border Radius**: SM (10px)

### Cards
- **Background**: Elevated white
- **Border**: 2px solid brand color
- **Border Bottom**: 4px (3D depth)
- **Border Radius**: LG to XL
- **Padding**: LG (16px)
- **Shadow**: Brand-colored shadow

### Badges
- **Background**: Brand colors
- **Padding**: SM horizontal, XS vertical
- **Border Radius**: Full (pill shape)
- **Shadow**: Subtle brand shadow
- **Text**: Label or Caption, bold

### Progress Bars
- **Height**: 5-8px
- **Background**: Light brand color
- **Fill**: Primary brand color
- **Border Radius**: Full
- **Animation**: Smooth spring animation

## Animation Principles

### Timing
```
Fast:      200ms  (Micro-interactions)
Normal:    300ms  (Standard transitions)
Slow:      400ms  (Emphasized changes)
Very Slow: 600ms  (Celebration moments)
```

### Easing
- **Entrance**: Spring (bouncy, welcoming)
- **Exit**: Ease-out (smooth, natural)
- **Interaction**: Ease-in-out (responsive)

### Haptics
- **Light**: Button taps, selections
- **Medium**: Success states, completions
- **Heavy**: Errors, warnings

## Accessibility Standards

### Touch Targets
- **Minimum**: 44x44px (WCAG AAA)
- **Comfortable**: 48x48px
- **Large**: 56x56px

### Contrast Ratios
- **Text**: 4.5:1 minimum (WCAG AA)
- **Large Text**: 3:1 minimum
- **Interactive**: 3:1 minimum

### Motion
- Respect `prefers-reduced-motion`
- Provide alternatives to animation-dependent feedback

## Best Practices

### DO ‚úÖ
- Use warm, encouraging language
- Celebrate every win, big or small
- Show progress and growth visibly
- Use brand colors consistently
- Maintain 3D depth with border bottoms
- Add haptic feedback to interactions
- Use smooth spring animations
- Keep touch targets 48px+
- Frame everything positively

### DON'T ‚ùå
- Use corporate or robotic language
- Show errors without helpful guidance
- Hide progress indicators
- Mix color meanings (gold = rewards always)
- Forget border bottoms on elevated elements
- Skip haptic feedback
- Use harsh linear animations
- Make touch targets < 44px
- Use negative framing
